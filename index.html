Architecture

Vision Encoder (Tiny ViT)
Image: 224×224 → 32×32 patches → 49 visual tokens
3 transformer layers, dim=192, 3 heads
Patch embedding via Conv2d + learned position embeddings
Language Decoder (Small GPT)
4 causal transformer layers, dim=256, 4 heads
Vocab: ~5014 tokens (14 special + 1000 location + 4000 words)
Autoregressive generation with causal masking
Multi-Task Output Format (Florence-2 style)
<caption> a dog on grass
<vqa> how many? <sep> 2
<od> dog <box>loc loc loc loc</box>
<seg> dog <poly>vertices...</poly>
Coordinates: 1000 bins (0.1% resolution). Loss: pure cross-entropy.
